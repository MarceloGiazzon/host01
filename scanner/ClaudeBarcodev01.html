Barcode Scanner

Camera Permission Required

To scan barcodes, please allow camera access:

  • Chrome/Edge: Click the camera icon in the address bar
  • Firefox: Click "Allow" when prompted
  • Safari: Go to Settings > Safari > Camera > Allow
  • Mobile: Check your browser settings for camera permissions

After allowing camera access, refresh the page and try again.

Position barcode within the frame

The camera will automatically scan when a barcode is detected
